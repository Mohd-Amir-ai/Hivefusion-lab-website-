Q: How do I reset the Pocket Mini? ▼
A: There’s a small button on the back of your device labeled RST. Just press it once, and your Pocket Mini will reset.
Q: How do I access the Pocket Mini’s website to start using attacks? ▼
A: First, connect your phone or laptop to the Pocket Mini’s Wi-Fi. Once connected, open any web browser (like Chrome) and type 10.0.0.1 in the address bar. That’s it—you’re in.
Q: What is HiveFusion Lab? ▼
A: HiveFusion Lab is an Indian ethical hardware development company. We create innovative ethical hacking hardware through R&D, designed specifically for the Indian market.
Q: How do I power on the device? ▼
A: You’ll need a standard 5V power supply. A regular mobile charger works, or you can use a Type-C to Type-C cable—connect one end to your phone and the other to the Pocket Mini. If it doesn’t power on, try a 5V adapter.
Q: How do I change the Pocket Mini’s device name? ▼
A: Connect to the Pocket Mini’s Wi-Fi, open 10.0.0.1 in your browser, tap the Settings icon, and change the device name there.
Q: How do I change the Pocket Mini’s Wi-Fi name? ▼
A: Same steps as changing the device name—connect to the Pocket Mini, go to 10.0.0.1, tap Settings, and update the Wi-Fi name.
Q: What are the default login details for the Pocket Mini? ▼
A: Wi-Fi Name (SSID): Pocket Mini Password: pocketMini
