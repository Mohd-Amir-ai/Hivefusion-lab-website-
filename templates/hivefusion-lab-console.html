HiveFusion Labs Pocket Mini

A compact , plug-and-play device built specifically to run Wi-Fi deauthentication and attack tools. Designed for learning and experimentation , it eliminates the need for GPIO pins , displays, or buttons. Just power it via Type-C and you're ready. No more flashing firmware on ESP8266 boards or wasting full microcontrollers. Portable, efficient, and affordable perfect for beginners and pros who want to explore Wi-Fi security with ease.
